Inscriptions for the Seeker
I.
The Core Instruments — Math AI: Conquer numbers. Solve, Explain, Graph, or Practice algebra to calculus. Essay Writer: Perfect your prose. Set your exact grade level (6th–Pro) and toggle the Humanizer. Study & Notes: Map concepts or transcribe lectures instantly.
II.
Verify & Grade — Double Check: Trust, but verify facts and cross-reference. Grading: Submit a rubric and let the AI score with detailed feedback. Test Prep: Train for ACTs and SATs with realistic problems.
III.
Productivity — Smart Scan (Quick Snap): Press Q to upload photos of textbooks or worksheets. Analytics & Streaks: Track XP, master topics, and maintain your daily flame.
IV.
Personal Intelligence — Complete the one-time quiz so the AI knows your grade, state, classes, and learning style. Every answer becomes tailored to your exact curriculum.
V.
The Golden Rules — Be specific in the chat, use the right tool for the job, and ALWAYS attach files (PDFs/Images) for context using the paperclip icon!
